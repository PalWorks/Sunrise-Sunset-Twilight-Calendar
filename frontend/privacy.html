Privacy Policy

At the Yoga Sadhana Calendar, we respect the absolute privacy of your daily spiritual routines. We believe that your astronomical and geographical locations are completely personal, and our platform is engineered to protect them accordingly.

Data Privacy Principles

We adhere to strict data privacy principles as outlined below:

  • No Location Tracking: Any coordinate data, latitude, longitude, or city searched inside our interface is handled purely client side in your local web browser. Your exact coordinates are never permanently saved on our servers or sold to third party platforms.
  • No Cookies: We do not set analytical, marketing, or tracking cookies inside your browser.
  • Subscription Privacy: Live webcal URL subscription requests calculate solar transits dynamically in memory when requested by your native device, without maintaining historical log records of your movements.

Third Party Libraries

Our mapping display uses Leaflet, open source OpenStreetMap tiles, and OpenStreetMap Nominatim reverse geocoding API to resolve coordinates. The queries are handled directly from your browser to their standard services without passing through intermediate databases.
